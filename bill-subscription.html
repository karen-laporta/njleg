Bill Subscription Service

View your bill subscription or to subscribe:

Helpful tips for navigating your bill subscriptions

The bill subscription service allows you to create and maintain a list of bills. This is a fast and easy way for you to track a group of bills throughout the legislative session. This free service is a time saver for users who track large numbers of bills. As a subscriber, you create a list of bills that enables you to retrieve the latest status and information for all of the bills on your list. You will be notified of any legislative action taken on the bill after a legislative session.
You can access the Bills Subscription Service link from the Bills section of the home page. When you first click on the link, you will get a log on box. Enter your e-mail address and then click the Submit button. When a message appears, "You do not have a subscription with this e-mail address," click on the Yes button to subscribe now. Your browser will then display the main bill subscription screen.

You may also create an account after performing an advanced search for the current legislative session and clicking on Show Bills Subscription button, you can enter your email address and click on OK to create an account or click on the Bills Subscription Service link to maintain an existing account.

You need to log on only once per session while you are using subscription list related pages.
Add
Using a Basic Search:
View the bill subscription screen and click on the Search Bills in Subscription Mode button. This will take you to the homepage where you can perform a search by Bill Number, Subject, Sponsor, Keyword, Last Date of Action, or Governor's Action. To add bill(s), click in the box for the bill number and click on the Subscribe button.

Using an Advanced Search:
Perform a search for the current legislative session using the Advanced Search button on the homepage. Click the Show Bills Subscription button above your search results. Enter your email address and click OK. You can now check the box next to bills that you want to add to your subscription list. Click the Subscribe button when you are finished selecting bills from your list of results. Click Exit to leave the subscription view.

View
View the bill subscription screen and click on the View/Modify List of Subscribed Bills button. You are now viewing your list of subscribed bills.

Remove
View the bill subscription screen and click in the box for the bill number to remove from list. Click on the Remove button at the top of the list.
The bill subscription service is a free service of the New Jersey Legislature Home Page. No guarantee of service or performance is given or implied. While the intent is to maintain this service throughout each New Jersey legislative session, it may be shutdown, or the addition of new service accounts may be stopped at any time.
