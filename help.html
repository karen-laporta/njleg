Members

Find your Legislator

Locate your legislator by using the Alphabetical List of Members, an alphabetical list by Municipality, a numeric list of Legislative Districts or an Interactive Map of Legislative Districts where you can click on the part of the state in which you live and it will display your legislators.

Alphabetical List of Members

All Senators are listed first, followed by Assemblymen/Assemblywomen. If you click on the legislator's name, you will be linked to his/her legislative home page.

Municipality

Allows you to click on the first letter of your municipality or scroll through the alphabetical list to locate it. Once you locate your municipality and click on the link it will show you all of the other municipalities that comprise your legislative district. If you then click on your district number you will be able to see the legislators for that district with their names being a link to their legislative home page.

Legislative District

Enables you to click on your district number. Once you click your district number you will see the municipalities in your district, and you can click on the district number again to view your list of legislators with their names being a link to their legislative home page.

Legislative Leadership

This shows you the leadership positions and who holds them for both houses of the legislature. The legislator's names are a link to their legislative home page.

Legislative Roster

This is a district list in numeric order that gives each legislator for a district, their party, and their district office address and telephone number. The legislator's names are a link to their legislative home page.

Seating Charts

This enables you to first choose whether you wish to view the Senate Seating Chart or the Assembly Seating Chart. Once you choose a link, a floor diagram of the chambers is displayed showing where each legislator sits with a link to their legislative home page.

Bills

Bills 2020-2021

You can search for a bill by Bill Number, Keyword(s), Bill Sponsor, Bill Subject, Bills in Committee, or Thesaurus. See explanations of each search type on the Bills page. Once the appropriate bill number is located you can click on the bill number to view the bill sponsors, bill history, and bill text links. Bill text is available in both PDF and HTML format. Click on the appropriate format bill link to view the bill text.

Bill Subscription Service

The subscription list is a feature that allows users to create and maintain a list of bills. This is a fast and easy way for subscribers to track a group of bills throughout the legislative session. This free service is a time saver for users who track large numbers of bills. As a subscriber, you create a list of bills that enables you to retrieve the latest status and information for all of the bills on your list.

Committees

Senate Committees

This provides a list of all the Senate committees. When you click on a committee link, a list of it's members will be displayed. When you click on the committee member's name it will take you to their legislative home page. If you click on the committee name link above the members list it will take you to a list of bills in that committee. When you click on a bill number, it will take you to a list of the bills sponsors, bill history, and links to the bill text.
The Committee Aides link below the Senate Committees link takes you to a list of all the Senate committees. When you click on a committee name it will show you the Republican, Democratic, and OLS Aide for that committee as well as their phone number. When you click on the OLS Aide's name it is a link to their e-mail.

Assembly Committees

This provides a list of all the Assembly committees. When you click on a committee link, a list of it's members will be displayed. When you click on the committee member's name it will take you to their legislative home page. If you click on the committee name link above the members list it will take you to a list of bills in that committee. When you click on a bill number, it will take you to a list of the bills sponsors, bill history, and links to the bill text.
The Committee Aides link below the Assembly Committees link takes you to a list of all the Assembly committees. When you click on a committee name it will show you the Republican, Democratic, and OLS Aide for that committee as well as their phone number. When you click on the OLS Aide's name it is a link to their e-mail.

Joint Committees

This takes you to a list of the Joint committees. When you click on a committee link, a list of it's members will be displayed. When you click on the committee member's name it will take you to their legislative home page. If you click o the committee name link above the members list it will take you to a list of bills in that committee. When you click on a bill number, it will take you to a list of the bills sponsors, bill history, and links to the bill text.

Districts

Districts by Number

This first gives you the opportunity to choose the session you wish to view and then you can click on your district number link. Once you click your district number, you will see the municipalities in your district, and you can click on the district number again to view your list of legislators. The legislator's names are a link to their legislative home page.

District Map

This first gives you the opportunity to choose the session you wish to view, and then you can see how the state is divided into legislative districts and the district numbers for each part of the state.

Municipalities Index

This first gives you the opportunity to choose the session you wish to view, and then it allows you to click on the first letter of your municipality or scroll through the list to locate it. Once you locate your municipality and click on the link it will show you all of the other municipalities that comprise your legislative district. If you then click on your district number you will be able to see the legislators for that district with their names being a link to their legislative home page.

Laws & Constitution

Statutes

This takes you to the title list of the New Jersey Statutes database. You can click on the plus sign to the left of a particular title to see all the statute sections that are within that title. You can then click on a statute section number link to see the statute text.
If you know the statute section number you are looking for you can type citation number enclosed in double quotes and click on the Search button. You can then click on the statute section number link to view the statute text.
You can also search by keyword by typing the word and then click on the Search button. If you are looking for a phrase be sure to enclose the search text in double quotes.

Chapter Laws

You first get the opportunity to click on the chapter laws by session year which gives you access to Chapter Laws and Joint Resolution for that two year period. Both the Chapter Laws and Joint Resolution have links to the bill they are regarding and the text of the law or resolution.
Also in this area, you can view a subject Index to the Chapter Laws by year. There is then a link to the Chapter Law text within the subject listing.

Constitution

This gives you the option to view the Complete Text of the New Jersey State Constitution or Search the Text of the New Jersey State Constitution.
When searching the Constitution, you can click on the plus sign to the left of a particular article to see the available sections with in that article. When you then click on the link to the section number and the section text will be displayed. You can also search by typing a keyword or phrase and then click on the Search button. If you are looking for a phrase be sure to enclose the search text in double quotes.

Legislative Publications

Budget & Finance

This takes you to links from the Legislative Budget and Finance office which shows general information regarding that unit. An Analysis of the Governor's Budget link gives you the opportunity to choose the fiscal year you want to view the analysis from. You can also access a summary of the Annual Appropriations Act as well as the OLS Revenue Snapshot, a brief report that includes a narrative summary of major tax collection trends in the fiscal year, and a table that compares revenues in the current fiscal year to the comparable amount received at the same point in the prior fiscal year.

Audit Reports

This is a link to the office of the State Auditor containing general office information as well as a list of annual reports, audit reports, and quarterly reports. Click on the link in the appropriate list to view the audit text. There is also a staff directory for the office of the State Auditor available on this page.

Legislative Reports

This gives a list of legislative reports issued by the Office of Legislative Services with a link to the report text.

Legislative Digest

You have access to the current Legislative Digest as well as past issues of the digest which is produced the day after a session of either house providing up to date information of legislative activity. You also have access to the Last Date of Action report which shows the status and history of any bills that were active on the specific date of the report.

Public Hearing Transcripts

This link allows you to view the full text of transcribed public hearings and meetings broken down by year. Once you click on the link for the year of the public hearings, you can then choose the hearing to view by clicking on the appropriate link.

Our Legislature

This takes you to a text explanation of how the New Jersey Legislature is designed. Information on legislators, districts, sessions, powers, etc. is all discussed in this area.

New Jersey Statutes

The New Jersey Statutes is the compilation of law that is the most current official text of the permanent and general statutes of New Jersey available without charge to the public pursuant to P.L. 1995, c. 319 (C. 52:11-78).

To search by statute citation, enter the citation in quotes. For example to search for the statute 52:11-78, enter "52:11-78" in the search box at the bottom of the search page.

To search by keywords or phrases, see the following examples:

  • building (single word search)
  • counselor and licensure (two or more words within a statute)
  • doctor or physician (either of two words within a statute)
  • insurance not automobile (one word but not another within a statute)
  • "water supply bond act" (exact phrase search)
  • educ* (wildcard to retrieve statutes for "educate," "education," "educator," etc.)
  • "Senate Assembly" /6 (words within 6 words of each other)

To browse using the Table of Contents (a list of the New Jersey Statutes by Titles), click on the plus sign appearing next to the Title desired.
