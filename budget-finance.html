Budget & Finance

Legislative Budget & Finance Office

The Legislative Budget and Finance Officer, as the chief fiscal officer for the Legislature and the Legislative Services Commission, prepares fiscal information for the Legislature and its budget committees, reviews requests for appropriations, and determines approval for the transfer of funds among State accounts. The Legislative Budget and Finance Office administers the fiscal note process and staffs the Joint Budget Oversight Committee.
